Recruiters Training Hub

Welcome to RTB | Recruiters Training Hub!

If you're interested in pursuing a career as a US IT recruiter, domestic IT recruiter, or non-IT recruiter, you've found the perfect destination. We provide comprehensive training programs for recruiters at all levels, catering to both newcomers and seasoned professionals looking to enhance their skills and thrive in the dynamic world of recruitment. We also offer support in finding employment until you secure a position.

Our training programs provide practical exposure in recruitment, with a focus on HR and sales. We offer comprehensive training in written and oral English communication, as well as placement assistance and on-the-job support. Recruiters are expected to have certain prerequisites, regardless of whether they are from IIT or non-IIT backgrounds. These prerequisites include having a degree, good communication skills in English, and a basic understanding of technical knowledge. Additionally, recruiters should be familiar with the topics that will be covered during refreshers. Effective communication, understanding office dynamics, developing soft skills and personality, crafting a strong resume, preparing for interviews and practicing with mock questions.
